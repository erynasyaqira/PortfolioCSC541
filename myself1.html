My passion for reading knows no bounds. I immerse myself in novels of various genres, building a personal library filled with both physical books and e-books. Always seeking new adventures, I eagerly anticipate events like book fairs and conventions, where I can indulge in my love for literature. One of my fondest memories is attending the annual Kuala Lumpur International Book Fair, where I discovered new authors and titles that expanded my literary horizons. My journey with books began in primary school, and since then, reading has been a constant source of joy and inspiration in my life.

When it comes to strengths, resilience is my secret weapon. Challenges don't scare me; they fuel my determination to grow and succeed. However, like many, I'm learning to tame my inner perfectionist and embrace imperfection as part of the journey.

My ultimate goal? Balancing a successful career with unwavering mental health. I believe that true success is not just about professional achievements but also about finding harmony and fulfillment in every aspect of life.

My values are my guiding stars: honesty, kindness, self-esteem, and effective communication. These principles shape my decisions and interactions, both personally and professionally.

  • Values: Honesty, Kindness, Self-esteem, Communicative

Self-care isn't just a buzzword for me—it's a non-negotiable. I prioritize my physical, mental, and emotional well-being through regular exercise, mindfulness practices, and quality time with loved ones. Flexibility and adaptability are key as I navigate life's twists and turns, ensuring my self-care remains effective and sustainable.
